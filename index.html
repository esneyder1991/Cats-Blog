Grooming and Hygiene

Regular grooming is important to keep your cat's coat healthy and free of mats and tangles. Brush your cat's fur regularly to remove loose hair and reduce shedding. Trim your cat's nails to prevent them from becoming overgrown and causing discomfort or injury. Additionally, clean your cat's ears and teeth regularly to prevent infections and dental problems.
